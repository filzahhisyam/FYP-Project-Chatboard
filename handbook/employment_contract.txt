Appointment:
For every new employee, they will receive an appointment letter from the HR department before starting their duties.

Employee Personal Information:
Employees should provide updated personal information for record purposes.

Pre-employment medical check-up:
All new employees must undergo a medical check-up before the letter of employment is issued.

Orientation Program:
onducted by the HR Department for all new employees to help them understand the Group's policies and benefit.

Departmental Orientation:
Conducted by the Head of SBU for the new Employee.

Probation and Confirmation:
- All permanent employees are required to serve a 6-month probation period from the date of appointment.
- Probationary period may be extended for up to another 6months if the performance does not meet the Group's standards
- The Group can terminate an employee if they do not fulfull expectations during or end of probation period.
