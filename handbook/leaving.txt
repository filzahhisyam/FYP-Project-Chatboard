Termination procedure: 
An employee can be terminated by the Group or can terminate their service by giving written notice or payment in lieu of notice.

Upon termination, the employee must surrender all Group property and settle any liabilities.

An employee is eligible to receive all unused accrued leave for the calendar year if they have complied with all exit procedures.

Termination of Employment:

- Either party may terminate the contract with a written notice or payment in lieu.
- The termination notice period is based on the following:
    - During Probation: 1 month.
    - M3 and above: 2 weeks.
    - E1 and below: 3 months.
    - Upon Confirmation: 
        - M3 and above: 3 month notice period
        - E1: 2 month notice period
        - E2 and below: 1 month.

- The employee who has resigned from the Group shall not be eligible for re-hiring three (3) years from his resignation except with the approval from the Senior Partner/Group CEO.

Job abandonment:
Employee that abandons job will be terminated immediately, and not eligible for rehire.

Retirement:
When employee reaches the official age for retirement at 55 years old on their birthday

Exit Interview:
 When an employee leaves the company, they may be asked to attend an "exit interview" with the Head of SBU
