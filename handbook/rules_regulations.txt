Disciplinary Action:
The Head of SBU or immediate superior may impose any one or
combination of the following disciplinary action against an employee: -
1. Verbal warning;
2. Written warning;
3. Final written warning;
4. Formal action, which will include but is not limited to:-
    - suspension with or without pay for a period of time;
    - Reduction of salary
    - Dismissal with contractual notice; and
    - Filing a legal suit against employee.
