Payment of salary is done on monthly basis, payments are made through the bank designated by the group

For income tax, the Group will make income tax deductions according to the latest tax schedule. 
Employees are responsible for settling their own salary tax liabilities unless otherwise specified.

For Employee Provident Fund (EPF), the Group contributes 12% of basic salary for all employees.
An additional 3% is contributed for employees who have completed more than five years of continuous service.

Social Security Contribution (SOCSO): The Group contributes to SOCSO as per the Employees' Social Security Organisation Act.

Annual increment and bonus payments are based on: 
- discretion of the group
- annual performance appraisal
- past performance improvement, dependability, attitude, cooperation, and disciplinary action taken
- adherence to all Employment Policies
- salary range of the employee's position.
