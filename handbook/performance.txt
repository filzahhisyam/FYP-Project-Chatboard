For performance appraisal, the group has a performance appraisal system to ensure all employees perform at their best. 
The system recognizes good performance and provides suggestions for improvement.

All employees are given Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) with detailed requirements and expectations.

Performance is evaluated via a performance appraisal. Appraisals are conducted at least once a year by the Head of SBU/Line Manager.

Performance Interview:
- The purpose of performance evaluation meeting is to:
    - Provide the Head of SBU/Line Manager and the employee an opportunity to discuss job performance during the previous performance cycle.
    - Plan for future performance objectives.
    - Ensure the evaluation meeting is a careful planning and preparation part of the Head of SBU/Line Managers and employee.
