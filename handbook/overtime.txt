For overtime employees with a salary of RM1,500.00 or less per month are entitled to claim overtime allowance.

The overtime rate is one and a half (1 1/2) times the hourly pay.
For the first hour, payment is calculated in units of 30 minutes, rounded to the nearest 30 minutes.

Time-Off-In-Lieu:
Applicable to employees earning RM1,501.00 and above.

General claim processes:
Employees can claim a meal allowance if they meet the overtime work conditions.
Overtime claims must be submitted by the 15th of each month to the Head of SBU/Line Manager for recommendation and then to the HR/Admin Department for verification before final approval from the Group CEO.
